Routers & Switches

A reliable and secure network starts with the right hardware

A reliable and secure network starts with the right hardware. Skytech provides expert consultation, installation, and configuration of Routers and Switches to ensure seamless connectivity, optimal performance, and strong network security for your business.

Whether you're setting up a new office network or upgrading an existing one, we ensure your routers and switches are configured for speed, stability, and future growth.

Our Services Include

Router Setup & Configuration

Installation of business-grade routers for fast, secure internet connectivity and efficient network traffic management.

Managed & Unmanaged Switches

Deployment of switches based on your business size and needs—whether it's a simple setup or a fully managed network.

Network Segmentation

Creating VLANs and network zones for improved performance and enhanced security.

QoS Configuration

Prioritizing critical applications (like VoIP or video conferencing) for smoother performance.

Firewall & Security Integration

Configuring routers with built-in firewalls and integrating with your broader security infrastructure.

Troubleshooting & Upgrades

Diagnosis and optimization of existing network issues, along with hardware upgrades for scalability.
