Complete IT Audit

Complete IT Audit services to evaluate the efficiency, security, and compliance of your IT infrastructure.

Skytech offers thorough and insightful Complete IT Audit services to evaluate the efficiency, security, and compliance of your IT infrastructure. Our audits help identify gaps, reduce risks, and ensure that your technology supports your business objectives effectively.

With Skytech’s Complete IT Audit, you gain the clarity and confidence to strengthen your digital foundation and plan for future growth.

Our IT Audit Covers

Infrastructure Review

Detailed analysis of hardware, servers, network components, and system configurations.

Security Assessment

Evaluation of cybersecurity measures, firewalls, antivirus systems, and user access controls.

Software Compliance

Verification of software licenses and identification of any unauthorized or outdated applications.

Data Protection & Backup

Review of data storage, backup routines, and disaster recovery readiness.

Network Performance

Testing of bandwidth, connectivity, and network efficiency.

Policy & Procedure Review

Assessment of IT policies, user protocols, and business continuity plans.
